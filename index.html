Catherine Ouriach

Catherine is a qualified Psychotherapist with over 17 years experience working in mental health.

Following her undergraduate degree in Psychology (BSc Hons), she completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy at the Institute of Psychiatry in 2010. She has worked in a variety of settings including the NHS, charity, the private health sector and has her own private practice. She has a special interest in Insomnia and is about to start a position in the Insomnia clinic at the internationally renowned Royal Hospital for Integrative Medicine based in Queens Square, London.

Experience and interests

  • Insomnia
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Post Traumatic Stress
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

Catherine is an empathic practitioner. She provides a safe and confidential space for clients online or face to face in North London to express concerning thoughts and feelings, gain deeper insight into difficulties and to develop positive strategies to move forward.
