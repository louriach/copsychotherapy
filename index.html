Education and interests

Following her undergraduate degree in Psychology (BSc Hons), she completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy at the Institute of Psychiatry in 2010.

She has worked in a variety of settings including the NHS, charity, the private health sector and has her own private practice.

Style

Catherine is an empathic practitioner. She provides a safe and confidential space for clients online or face to face in North London to express concerning thoughts and feelings, gain deeper insight into difficulties and to develop positive strategies to move forward.
